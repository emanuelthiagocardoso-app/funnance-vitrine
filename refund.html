Refund Policy for FUNNANCE

1. Free Trial Period: FUNNANCE offers a generous 45-day free trial for all new users to explore and test the platform without any financial commitment.

2. Subscription Renewals and Refunds: Users are charged 5.99 € per year automatically after the 45-day free trial ends, unless canceled. Due to the digital nature of the software and the availability of the long free trial period, subscription fees are generally non-refundable once processed.

3. Cancellation: You can cancel your annual subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, you will retain access to the application's features until the end of your paid billing annual period.

4. Contact Us: For any billing inquiries or exceptional support requests, please contact us at contact@funnance.app.
